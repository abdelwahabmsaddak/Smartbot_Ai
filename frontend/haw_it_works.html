How SmartBot Works

SmartBot provides tools for analyzing digital assets, gold, and selected stocks, along with an integrated private chat system and a simple dashboard.

Step 1 — Create an Account

You sign up and instantly receive your free 1-month trial.

Step 2 — Choose a Market

Select a currency, gold, or stock to receive a full market overview.

Step 3 — Chat Page

Enjoy a dedicated interactive chat directly inside the platform.

Step 4 — Price Tracking

Track the market and receive instant notifications.
